Larry-the cat

Introduction

Larry is the world-famous resident of 10 Downing Street, serving as the official Chief Mouser to the Cabinet Office since 2011.

Crime

While internet memes often portray him as a "crime lord" ruling Whitehall, his only true offenses involve naps and occasional territory disputes with neighboring cats.

Duty in jail

If he were ever tasked with prison duty, his primary role would likely involve patrolling the facility for mice and providing emotional support to the inmates, though he would certainly insist on having the final say on all interior design choices.
