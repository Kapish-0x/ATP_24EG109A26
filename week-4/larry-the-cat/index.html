Larry

about:

Larry the Cat is the famous resident feline of 10 Downing Street, serving as the Chief Mouser to the Cabinet Office since 2011. A brown-and-white tabby, he is known for his calm demeanor, occasional hunting exploits, and symbolic role in British politics. Though officially tasked with pest control, Larry has become a beloved public figure, often spotted lounging outside the Prime Minister’s residence and charming visitors with his presence.
